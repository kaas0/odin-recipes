Stew of Lamb and Barley

Description

A Viking-style lamb and barley stew would have been a nourishing dish during long feasts, perfect for Odin's warriors in Valhalla.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lamb (cubed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 carrots (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup barley
  • 4 cups vegetable or beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown the lamb cubes in a bit of oil.
  2. Add onions, carrots, and garlic, saut√©ing for a few minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the barley, broth, thyme, and bay leaf.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours until the lamb is tender and the barley is cooked.
  5. Season with salt and pepper before serving.
