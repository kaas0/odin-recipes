Skyr with Honey and Berries

Description

Skyr is an Icelandic dairy product similar to yogurt, consumed by the Norse people. Odin may have enjoyed this as a light, refreshing dish with honey and wild berries.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups skyr (or Greek yogurt)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 cup fresh berries (blueberries, blackberries, or raspberries)
  • 2 tablespoons crushed nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Divide the skyr into bowls.
  2. Drizzle honey over the skyr.
  3. Add fresh berries on top, and if desired, sprinkle with crushed nuts.
  4. Serve chilled.
