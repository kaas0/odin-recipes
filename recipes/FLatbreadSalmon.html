Flatbread with Smoked Salmon

Description

Flatbread was a staple in Viking cuisine, often paired with fish. Odin may have enjoyed flatbread topped with smoked salmon or other preserved fish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rye flour
  • 1 cup barley flour
  • Â½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 200g smoked salmon
  • Fresh dill (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix the flours and salt in a bowl. Gradually add water and olive oil, kneading until you form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each one out into thin rounds.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook each flatbread for about 2 minutes on each side until browned and firm.
  4. Let the flatbreads cool slightly before topping them with smoked salmon and fresh dill.
  5. Serve as an appetizer or side dish.
