Mead

Description

Mead, a fermented honey drink, was Odin’s preferred drink according to many myths. The recipe below shows how to make a basic mead at home.

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs honey
  • 1 gallon of water
  • 1 packet of wine yeast (or champagne yeast)
  • A handful of raisins
  • Spices (optional: cinnamon, cloves, or ginger)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat the water (don’t boil), and stir in the honey until fully dissolved.
  2. Once the mixture cools down to room temperature, pour it into a sanitized fermenting container.
  3. Add the yeast and raisins (they help fermentation) and any optional spices for flavor.
  4. Seal the container with an airlock and let it ferment for 3-4 weeks in a dark, cool place.
  5. After fermentation, siphon the mead into bottles and let it age for at least 6 months for the best flavor.
