Roast Boar

Description

In Valhalla, Odin’s warriors feast on the ever-replenishing boar Sæhrímnir. Here's a simple recipe for roast boar, which the mythic warriors might have eaten.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs of wild boar (or pork if boar is unavailable)
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Fresh rosemary and thyme
  • 1 cup mead (or red wine)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rub the boar with salt, pepper, minced garlic, and herbs.
  3. In a bowl, mix honey, mustard, and mead. Pour this mixture over the boar.
  4. Place the boar in a roasting pan and roast for 1.5 to 2 hours, basting occasionally with the pan juices.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
